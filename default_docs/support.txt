NovaAI provides multiple support channels for customers.

Support Options:
- Starter Plan: Community forum
- Pro Plan: Email support with 24-hour SLA
- Enterprise Plan: 24/7 dedicated support via phone, chat, and email

Contact Information:
- General inquiries: hello@novaai.com
- Technical support: support@novaai.com
- Billing issues: billing@novaai.com

Escalations:
Enterprise customers can request escalation for critical outages. A dedicated support engineer will respond within 1 hour.

Account Assistance:
To delete an account, navigate to Account Settings > Security > Delete Account. Note that all data and unused credits will be lost.
