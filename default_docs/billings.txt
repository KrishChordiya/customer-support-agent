Payment Methods:
NovaAI accepts Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and PayPal. Enterprise customers may request invoicing with net-30 payment terms.

Billing Cycle:
All subscriptions are billed monthly at the beginning of the billing cycle. Enterprise customers may request annual billing.

Refund Policy:
Refunds are available only if requested within 7 days of the initial purchase and if usage does not exceed 10% of the allocated tokens. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Failed Payments:
If a payment fails, the account will be placed on hold after 7 days. API access will be suspended until payment is resolved.

Cancellation:
Customers can cancel subscriptions anytime from the NovaAI dashboard. Accounts remain active until the end of the billing period.
